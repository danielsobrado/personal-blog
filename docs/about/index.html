About me

Hi!, I’m Daniel, originally from northern Spain and currently residing in Dubai where I take care of my little family: my beautiful wife and our 2 kids.

I enjoy traveling. Each and every time, I find something magical about having a new stamp on my passport. Apart from experiencing the beauty of a new place, I enjoy the experience wherein I get to meet new people, learn about their culture and traditions, and thus expand my worldview. Learning through travel changes you. It’s like you unravel and then re-emerge as a completely different person.

Writing and learning

I’m using this site as a tool for self-development. They say you never fully understand a subject until you can explain it. I believe each person views things from different angles, providing perspectives that may contrast but are nevertheless worth sharing. I write about topics I’m passionate about.

The opinions expressed herein are my own personal opinions and do not represent my employer’ s view in anyway. Thanks for visiting my page!
